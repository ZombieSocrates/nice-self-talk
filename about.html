What is this site?

PepTalk Bismol is an app that protects you from the person who is often your worst critic: yourself. If you give PepTalk Bismol permission to use your microphone and leave it open in your browser, it will use open-source speech recognition services to listen for negative self-talk. If PepTalk Bismol hears you put yourself down, it will speak up to reassure you and create a moment for positive self-reflection.

PepTalk Bismol was created in response to IDEO's DesignsOn Safety brief to address the question: How Might We fight the influence of negative self-talk in daily life, create space for self care and self reflection, and empower people to see themselves as others see them?
